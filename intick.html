Intick — Futures Trading, Reimagined

Back to Journal 

Intick is an innovative 0-to-1 futures trading platform designed to bring simplicity and speed to complex financial markets. Built from scratch, it focuses on making futures trading intuitive.
This project challenged me to rethink how traders interact with fast-moving markets, balancing powerful features with a clean, accessible interface. The result is a platform that empowers users to act confidently and quickly, helping them stay ahead in a dynamic environment.
